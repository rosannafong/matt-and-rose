Matthew's and Rosanna's Story

This is a story about Matthew and Rosanna

How they met

Matthew and Rosanna met in chess club at Massey University Palmerston North in 2007.

Rosanna was quite shy at the time, but felt welcomed by Matthew as he was a really friendly guy.

They started talking a lot...

They became good friends

Matthew started inviting Rosanna around to his place for computer gaming sessions and they became good friends.

They both laughed a lot and had a lot of fun!

They really enjoyed each other's company.

They started dating

During 2008, Matthew asked Rosanna on a date with him.

He introduced her to bubble tea and she liked it!

They moved in together

In 2009, Matthew and Rosanna moved into a small little flat together.

They continued to go out on awesome dates.

They even went on vacations together.

They moved to Wellington

In 2010, they moved to Wellington together where Matthew started a new job as a software developer and Rosanna explored her creative side by studying Media Design at Victoria University of Wellington.

Not too long after, they were also given a pet cat. They named him Watson.

Rosanna graduated

In 2013, Rosanna graduated with a degree in Bachelor of Design Innovation and Matthew was there to support her.

They got engaged

During November 2013, they went on a trip to Bali where Matthew proposed to Rosanna on a sandy beach.

Rosanna said "Yes" and were engaged!

They are very happy

Now that they are engaged, they continue each day with love, friendship, shared interests, and funny jokes.

They are currently living as a happy couple with their cat Watson.

We look forward to seeing you at our wedding.
